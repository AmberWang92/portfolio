About Me

Hi, I’m Xinyue Wang — a Game Developer who loves turning wild ideas into playable worlds.

I design with empathy (yes, I overthink how players feel), think a little outside the box (sometimes I move the box entirely), and take full ownership from concept to code. I enjoy connecting big design ideas with real implementation — making sure the “cool vision” actually works in-game.

With a strong aesthetic sense and a soft spot for storytelling, I build experiences that are immersive, thoughtful, and just a little unexpected.

Welcome to my creative playground 🎮✨
