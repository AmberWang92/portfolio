Until the Fields: Farmageddon

Unity C# 3D Puzzle Platformer

About the Project

Until the Fields: Farmageddon is a 3D single-player puzzle platformer adventure where you swap between 3 animals: mouse, spider and cow — each with their own personality and tricks — Our heroes must cooperate to solve puzzles and escape the storm-wrecked barn!


The game is a student project developed by a team of 6 women over 3.5 months.


My Contributions

  • Core gameplay architecture and systems integration
  • Performance profiling and optimization passes
  • Tooling for faster content iteration
  • Puzzle blockouts to final art pass handoff
  • Scripting of interactive elements and triggers
  • Playtesting and iteration based on feedback
  • Managed Steam deployment workflow (store page setup, build uploads, version control, testing branches)
  • Release checklists and QA sign‑off
  • Build automation and versioning
